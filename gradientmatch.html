What are your privacy rights? Some regions have different privacy rights, but all users have the ability change their password or delete their account freely. Feel free to contact us at frogkissgames@gmail.com if you would like to have more access or control over your data.

If you do not wish to create an account, you may use our service as a guest. Third party services may have their own controls if they offer do-not-track features.

Do we make updates to this policy? We will update this policy as necessary and the updated version will be effective as soon as it's accessible. If we make material changes to this policy, we may notify you, but you are encouraged to review this policy to stay informed of how we are protecting your information.

How can you contact us about this policy? You may contact us at frogkissgames@gmail.com with any questions or comments about this policy.

Our application allows you to change your password or delete your account along with your data. For more access to your information, you may contact us at frogkissgames@gmail.com.

CCPA Privacy Policy

The California Code of Regulations defines a 'resident' as:
  1. every individual who is in the State of California for other than a temporary or transitory purpose and
  2. every individual who is domiciled in the State of California who is outside the State of California for a temporary or transitory purpose

All other individuals are defined as 'non-residents'.

If this definition of 'resident' applies to you, we must adhere to certain rights and obligations regarding your personal information.
